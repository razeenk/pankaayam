Rules & Safety

Your safety is our top priority. Please adhere to the following guidelines to ensure a fun and secure experience for everyone:

  • All participants must wear a provided life vest at all times.
  • Follow the instructions of your guide. They are experienced and trained for your safety.
  • No alcohol is permitted before or during the kayaking tour.
  • Stay with the group and do not wander off on your own.
